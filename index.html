The core concepts

There are some concepts being crucial for the entire narrative.

The first one includes energy, intellect and mind. It defines who HS is in whole and as a certain person particularly.

The second references to political power, political system and ideology. These, in turn, define what is the society.

And there is another immensely important factor — the scientific and technological progress. An interaction of all these three things defines what the civilization is and what is going to happen to it.

There is nothing new and too exciting here so far of course. But what is presumably is really interesting the new way of thinking of the source of so-called human nature and how it shapes the society itself. Nowadays, however, an opposite standpoint is widely propagated, that society forms human mindset. No doubt, it does, but only to some extent, not totally. And this misconception impedes many intellectuals to realize, why the modern society is not what humanists and liberals desire it to be, and never will. It explains why authoritarian leaders like Trump, Putin, Erdogan are as successful as their forerunners like Hitler or Stalin. By other words — why history is failed to be a good teacher for people.

And without understanding a true nature of HS species as not only a social, but a biological unit not in last turn, we are in danger never enter the future, which a huge part of our book is dedicated to as well. Instead of this, we are going to be doomed to go endlessly and grievously on closed circle when social progress suddenly ceased, crushing civilization and precipitate humanity into a new barbaric epoch.

Homo Sapiens decomposed

Born with legacy

There a concept of different segments of Homo Sapiens within the same species is established. We define them as:

  1. Homo Sapiens Re-active, HS(r)
  2. Homo Sapiens Thinking, HS(t) and
  3. Homo Sapiens Stirring, HS(s)

In our classification, at the certain point we refer to the popular concept named The Spiral Dynamics. But we go further, claiming that there are genetically predefined mental features for people which define the last stage an individual is able to reach.

  • Looking ahead, it is necessary to point out, that we strongly claim to exclude all kind of discrimination towards any person whatever his/her innate features and predispositions would be. This should be proclaimed absolutely clear. Moreover, further, we are going to consider how it is possible for people to have a meaningful (or happy if you prefer this term) life, despite all the differences of dignity and mental capacities which they have. Particularly there is intended to be a part devoted to the Human Enhancement subject, which becomes more and more important as society is affected by the accelerating pace of technical progress.

The idea is that their perception of reality and, consequently, behavior is defined by specific for a certain category so-called a discrete mentally-behavioral pattern (MBP). Put it simply, human, belonging to one category, never does some things which may be done by human from other one as their MBPs don’t intersect. Those MBPs, metaphorically speaking, ran in the parallel isolated containers.

For example, consider an extreme situation when people find themselves beyond any civilized social rules and constrains, oppressing their primordial instincts. It may be some natural disaster, war, imprisonment and so forth. It is well known that individuals divide instantly and spontaneously into two major groups, members of which conduct accordingly to the absolutely different behavioral models.

The first one implements the exact scenario innate to the primordial society of hunter-gatherers with all its attributes. The main force for that group is the brutal violence, rigid hierarchy of power and considering all other groups as rivals for resources and thereby enemies.

The second one contains members, denying such rules. They tend to support each other and their community relays on some moral imperatives. Although this doesn’t mean that there are no any authorities at all. But the crucial difference is that there is a proclivity to take into account interests of all members, including those who is weak and vulnerable. Empathy, unlikely the first group, plays role here.

Notice, that we consider the situation when the environment is the same for all those people. So why they conduct so differently? Because of their MBPs. They are genetically predefined. That means that someone will never become a looter, a murderer or a rapist, but someone unavoidably will under certain circumstances. This explains an immense amount of atrocities in situations mentioned above. When there is no restraining fear of the punishment, the real nature of such brutish human being appears.

And there is an explicit evolutionary reason for that, regarding us as a species. In fact, all the peculiarities in human nature have meaning as an inner variety is very important for species for their sustainability. The environment may change faster than species can evolve, so marginal specimen suddenly may fit new external requirements better than stable majority. And this outlines some biological framework which defines sets of mentally-behavioral features for the entire species for survival. Such idea is expressed in the discipline developed by E. O. Wilson named “Sociobiology”. This convergent scientific approach argues that living creatures have some evolutionary predefined behavioral patterns produced by natural selection which facilitates them to survive by the most effective way. It is very important to notice, that those patterns are different for species in whole and for a species unit. If, for some reason, they contradict to each other, then the species needs have priority. It sheds light on such peculiar apparitions in the nature like altruism and self-sacrificing. Put it simply, we often conduct unexpectedly to ourselves.

The idea about some predefined faculties which may overcome social demands is still considered as controversial by many modern intellectuals and even as heretical one. Especially if it regards not to obvious features like physical conditions but to moral ones. It is still widely assumed, accordingly to notorious the “tabula rasa” concept, that human being are born without any ethics at all and it is society which forms their beliefs and makes them morally responsible. It is well known how much suffering humanity endured due to such the utopic idea. The bloodiest ideology, communism, claimed to create a “new human” instead of just human and this has led to dozens of millions being killed, imprisoned and humiliated.

Actually, we don’t claim that someone is born fully fledged, either being a villain or a man of virtue. Moral imperatives are subject of human experience as well, but only to some extent. So again — someone will never do some things which someone other will. Hitler will never be Socrates and vice versa. First one was born not Hitler, of course, but with a set of features which led him to become Hitler under certain circumstances. The same about second one.

It appears from very childhood and pedagogues are aware of that very well. We know that children may differ much regarding their behavioral models from ethical point of view. Some of them like torturing animals or destroying what other children build. Moreover, evidently it may give them pleasure.

Let me repeat that we are considering biological predefinitions of diverse types of HS, but not any responsibility of them. The human being cannot bear any responsibility due to its nature or certain set of features. He/she is responsible only for acting and subsequences, but this is an absolutely different question.

In general terms, visible outcomes of different MBPs on the individual scale are:

  • The threshold of abstinence from violence
  • The exposure to propaganda
  • Ability to control emotionally driven behaviors
  • Capacity for strategic altruism
  • Predisposition to cooperation

Evidently those faculties greatly impact an individual behavior and all together with cultural norms applied to the particular society, defines what it is, what it is going to be and what it cannot be. We argue that not only MBPs are predefined, but ratio of segments within HS species is the same among all races and ethnicities across the globe. Our classification has nothing in common with any external differences of people and/or their societies (but the communities are absolutely another thing!). No matter if a particular human being is a man or a woman, white, black or Asian, which land he/she was born in etc.

HS and Civilization

Our classification assists to understand the essence of the most valuable problems that our society is faced on the global scale and how we could cope with them. Both the basic functional structure of any society and its driving forces as well, remain the same throughout the whole history of mankind. And this undoubtedly corresponds to the internal structure of HS as a species, although it is not a precise reflection of it yet.

It is established as a pyramid consisting of 3 layers. The basic one is masses, passively holding biological vigor, which they are unable to manage for any — more or less — sophisticated activity for sake of themselves, but which may be spontaneously released and cause of damage on social order. That what happens while revolutions, riots and natural disasters. In words of one syllable, they are a source of the primordial energy which we inherited from the animal kingdom. Further, we will use to call this the “relic energy of masses” (REM).

On the top of the pyramid, the elites reside. Those individuals possess a higher intellect, willing, and ambitions. As a rule, they are obsessed by obtaining the power. They are able to extract REM, coagulate it and use in name of getting power. And this often reshapes the society.

And between them, a middle tier lays — the functional apparatus. Its representatives possess intelligence, but not so bright as elites have. Also, they have no such great and focused on the power, willing. As a result, they are constrained to serve to elites as a tool to manage masses.

So the masses are HS(r) and partially — HS(s,d). The apparatus is HS(s,d) and HS(s,u), the elite is HS(s,u). Inasmuch it is about power, we don’t place HS(t) into this specific image. That kind of individuals keeps them aside of a direct political power as their mindset is different. They are driven by a classical philosophical representation of reality, considering the world of human beings through such absolutistic categories as “good” and “evil”, revealing a metaphysical (or, if you wish, a cosmological one) phenomenon, albeit some of them may do that even unconsciously. As a matter of fact, it is nothing else, but their ideas and/or deeds change the essence of society, although it may take centuries before they conquer up human minds (Socrates or Jesus would be perfect examples of such kind of people). So, they don’t want a political power, they desire an idea about a bsolute truth to took over the people.

But the point is that only elites, being highly intellectually developed, are capable to grasp the meaning of those ideas and realize the necessity of them to be applied for society. Masses cannot really understand anything beyond their biologically predefined, limited sensations. But because elites, obtaining power, need the vigor of masses, like a rocket requires an enormous amount of fuel to get into space, they always used to expose ideas produced by HS(t) in simplified version. This kind of idea is adapted to the masses mindset, persuading them that they are going to obtain something really important, even to such extent, that it deserves to sacrifice their lives.
